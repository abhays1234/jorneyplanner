Privacy Policy

At High End Travel, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information, and your rights in relation to that information.

What personal information do we collect?

We may collect personal information from you when you make a booking or enquiry with us, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our website or social media channels. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

How do we use your personal information?

We use your personal information to provide you with our products and services, to communicate with you about your bookings and enquiries, and to send you marketing materials if you have subscribed to our newsletter or given us permission to do so.

Who do we disclose your personal information to?

We may disclose your personal information to our third-party service providers, such as our payment gateway provider or marketing platform. We may also disclose your information to regulatory authorities if required to do so by law.

How do we protect your personal information?

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. This includes using secure sockets layer (SSL) encryption to transmit sensitive information such as payment details.

Your rights in relation to your personal information

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we hold. You may also withdraw your consent to us using your personal information for marketing purposes at any time.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, or if you would like to exercise your rights in relation to your personal information, please contact us at privacy@highendtravel.com.
